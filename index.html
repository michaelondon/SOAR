Welcome to SOAR

SOAR-AAPN is an organization for parents of students of African descent interested in continuing New Albany's diversity efforts and works to foster a more inclusive school community.This is done through services, outreach, and educational cultural and philanthropic endeavors.

SOAR-AAPN often hosts discussion forums and activities that promote leadership and community building. SOAR-AAPN is also involved in helping plan school events for MLK Day and Black History Month.
